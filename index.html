About Chelsea FC

Our History

Founded in 1905, Chelsea FC has grown from a local London club to one of the most successful and recognizable football clubs in the world. With over 100 years of rich history, we've become synonymous with excellence, passion, and the beautiful game.

Stamford Bridge

Our iconic home since 1905, Stamford Bridge is one of football's most historic stadiums. Located in Fulham, West London, it seats over 40,000 passionate fans who create an atmosphere unmatched anywhere in the world.

Our Fans

The Chelsea faithful are the heartbeat of our club. With millions of supporters worldwide, our fans create an electric atmosphere at every match and stand by us through every triumph and challenge.
